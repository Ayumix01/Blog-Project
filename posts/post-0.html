What is Virtual Reality?



Virtual Reality is a fully digital world usually accesible by a Head Mounted Dispay (HMD) such as the Meta Quest series of headsets or the Valve Index.

Virtual Reality is a 100% digital experience. Imagine you've stepped inside of a video game where you can look all around  and view the digital world with no real world interferance.

The most commonly known and used method of experiencing Virtual Reality is through a Head Mounted Display but there are other methods in which you can experience VR. One of those methods is through a fully immersive projection mapped space, although those experiences aren't very common outside of theme parks.

Other than gaming, one excellent use case for VR is meditation. There are several meditation apps such as TrippVR which are excellent methods of relaxation and zoning out. Some apps even offer guided meditations in serene environments like water falls or a psychedelic experience.

Another wonderful use case is teleconferencing and virtual office spaces. Being able to sit in a conference room from your own home while being fully embodied in a simulation brings teams closer together despite being far apart.
