VR on the Web



A not-so-commonly known fact amongst VR enthusiasts is that you can create fully immersive websites which you could access directly from the web browser on your favorite modern HMD.

Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality on the web offers an excellent opportunity to be able to experience websites in creative new ways! Imagine Portal hopping from one world to the next all within your HMD's browser.

Another neat thing about WebXR is that you can also experience it on pancake mode on tablet, mobile and PC web browsers.
